# Velo CRM — Systematic Product Audit (Pre-Saif-Dental Onboarding)

**Date:** 2026-06-26
**Auditor stance:** Clinic **owner** of Saif Dental — 4 doctors (Saif, Hawkar, +2), an X-ray department, and reception — evaluating Velo before a mid-July 2026 go-live, **migrating off GoHighLevel (GHL)** with the expectation that Velo is **"better than GHL."**
**Mode:** Read-only. No code changed. USER-level product audit — product gaps, not code quality / tech debt.
**Method:** 4 parallel deep-read agents covering (a) all `src/pages/*` + App.jsx-embedded surfaces, (b) all `src/components/*`, (c) all 12 `api/*` endpoints + `src/lib/*` data layer, (d) end-to-end clinical/operational workflows. Contested findings were re-verified by direct source inspection (file:line refs throughout).

---

## 1. Executive summary

**The clinical core is genuinely strong and is Velo's competitive moat.** Dental charting (per-tooth/surface findings, FDI/Palmer), treatment plans, prescriptions with print, clinical notes, documents, per-patient payments, and the **X-ray module + `xray_tech` role** are all real, DB-backed, org-scoped, audit-logged, bilingual (EN/AR), and correctly role-gated. **No competitor Saif is leaving (GHL) has any of this.** The X-ray workflow is the single most polished part of the product.

**But the features Saif depends on GHL for *every single day* are mock, broken in production, or built against a deleted schema.** Automated reminders, 2-way messaging, follow-up/recall sequences, contact import, and "who owes me money" all fail today. **Bluntly: migrating Saif right now would be a daily-operations *regression* on his front-desk and communication workflows, even though the clinical depth is a major upgrade.**

The failure patterns cluster into five root causes (fix these and ~20 findings collapse):

1. **Fake demo shells never replaced** — Inbox, Automations, Integrations, and Forms-publish are non-functional but presented as working. These are *exactly* GHL's headline features, shipped hollow.
2. **Silent data loss** — `sanitizeOrgUpdate`'s field whitelist drops WhatsApp/Meta/brand/AI settings while the UI flashes "Saved!".
3. **localStorage-as-database** — Tasks, Goals, Docs, Forms, notifications, clinic hours are per-browser/per-device, unshared across a 6-seat team.
4. **Client-side-only search over a 100-row page** — patient lookup collapses at Saif's 3,171-contact scale.
5. **Nav-only role gating** — page access is filtered only in the sidebar; direct URL / Cmd+K bypasses it.

**Bottom line for the July decision:** the clinical modules are ready. The front-desk/communications/reporting layer needs a focused pre-onboarding sprint, plus several product decisions from Ali (below) and a confrontation with the **Vercel 12/12 function ceiling** (automated reminders and public form submission each need a new function).

## 3. Findings by area

Each finding: **[ID] Severity / Effort** — description (file ref). "SB-#" cross-references the blocker table.

### 3.1 Clinical

**What works well (lean on these):**
- Per-tooth/surface charting, 10 finding types, FDI/Palmer notation (`dental.js:260`, `DentalTabs.jsx:1195`).
- **Treatment plans are fully shipped** — proposed→accepted→completed, line items, cost estimates (`schema.sql:245–278`, `dental.js:328–518`). *Memory correction: some memory notes list treatment plans as V1.5 backlog — they are live; verify and update memory.*
- Prescriptions with print + pre-printed-pad fallback; clinical notes; documents (real `documents` table + bucket); X-rays end-to-end (multi-file upload, HEIC→JPEG, lightbox, link-to-tooth + link-to-plan).
- Clinical write-gating is correct: `EDIT_ROLES = owner/doctor`; Documents widens to include receptionist; X-rays include `xray_tech`. Read-only roles get explicit banners.

**Gaps:**
- **[C-1] CRITICAL feature / L–XL — No recall / reactivation system.** No "due for checkup" or "not seen in N months" engine. Only vocabulary exists (`recall_due` enum, `recall` form type) with nothing behind it. This is the **single highest-value dental retention feature** and every competitor has it. *(Not a hard July blocker if Saif does recalls manually today, but it's the biggest "limits growth" gap.)*
- **[C-2] HIGH / L — Charted finding does not become a plan procedure; no fee schedule.** Procedures are free text, prices hand-keyed (`DentalTabs.jsx:1120`). 4 doctors will produce inconsistent procedure names/prices within weeks.
- **[C-3] HIGH / M — No clinical→billing link on the chart.** Completing a procedure creates no charge; the Treatment Plan tab shows a total but **no paid/balance**. Feeds SB-6.
- **[C-4] HIGH / M — Patient registration is thin.** Captures name/phone/email/dob/gender/allergies/doctor only. **No address, no insurance field**, and a `medical_history` column exists with no form field (dead capacity).
- **[C-5] MEDIUM / L — No encounter/visit entity.** Chart, notes, Rx, payment are 4 independent rows; staff can't pull up "the 24 June visit" as a unit.
- **[C-6] LOW — X-ray links to a plan, not to an individual procedure/tooth-item.**

### 3.2 Front Desk

**What works well:** Appointment booking is solid — patient/doctor/date/time/duration/type/notes, day+week+mini-calendar handling 50+/day across 4 doctor columns, 6-status lifecycle, conflict detection (`AppointmentsPage.jsx:676–1287`). Inline new-patient creation from the booking modal. Patients list has real server-side pagination + total count and good empty states.

**Gaps:**
- **[F-1] SB-2 / SB-3 / SB-5** — patient search at scale; dead WhatsApp inbox; manual `wa.me` reminder (see blocker table).
- **[F-2] HIGH / M — No real check-in / waiting-room flow.** "Check-in" just maps to `in_progress`; no arrival queue.
- **[F-3] HIGH / M — No no-show automation.** Manual status flip only; no auto-mark, no patient no-show counter, no rebooking trigger.
- **[F-4] HIGH / — Optimistic appointment status/delete/create don't revert on DB error** → phantom appointments and false "cancelled" state until refresh (`AppointmentsPage.jsx`). Looks like data loss to staff.
- **[F-5] SB-13 — Tasks localStorage-only**; also assignee/comment-author are free-text, not profile refs; no role gating (xray_tech should have no task access per MATRIX).
- **[F-6] MEDIUM / M — AddAppointmentModal is English-only** and forced `dir="ltr"` (`AddAppointmentModal.jsx:199,218–349`) — the most-used front-desk modal isn't Arabic, jarring for an Arabic-first clinic.
- **[F-7] MEDIUM — Day view silently drops appointments before 8am / after 8:30pm** (`topPx<0`). Two divergent "New Appointment" modals with different capabilities.
- **[F-8] HIGH / L — NotificationCenter only ever fires "new patient added"** (`App.jsx:700`); no appointment/no-show/payment/inbound-message alerts despite advertised types. Receptionist's most-wanted alerts are absent; also localStorage-only/per-device.
- **[F-9] MISSING — recurring appointments; chair/operatory assignment.** Booking knows the doctor but not the chair; with 4 doctors + X-ray sharing physical chairs, nothing prevents double-booking a room.

### 3.3 Reporting

**What works well:** ReportsPage is the strongest non-clinical page — real, org-scoped, currency-correct: revenue by month (per currency), appointments by status, top procedures, new patients/month, 90-day retention (`reports.js`, `ReportsPage.jsx`). Good empty/loading/error states.

**Gaps:**
- **[R-1] SB-7 — No per-doctor production report** (Saif's core ask).
- **[R-2] SB-6 — No outstanding-balance / A/R report** (blocked by the missing balance concept).
- **[R-3] MEDIUM — Missing owner-critical reports:** treatment-plan acceptance rate, recall effectiveness, no-show **rate** (shown as count, not %).
- **[R-4] MEDIUM / — GoalsPage half-real:** progress is live from Supabase, but goal **definitions are localStorage-only and not org-namespaced** → unshared, cross-tenant bleed risk under impersonation, and the doctor "read-only view" shows the doctor's own empty localStorage, not the owner's goals (`goals.js:4–7`).
- **[R-5] LOW/MEDIUM — Expenses table exists but is unused** (`schema.sql:317`); no P&L / expense tracking. FinancePage explicitly has no invoice/pending concept.
- **[R-6] LOW — ReportBuilder is an honest "coming soon" stub** (good pattern), but is effectively unreachable (ReportsPage no longer wires the open button).

### 3.4 Admin

**What works well:** The invite flow is real and supports all 4 invitable roles incl. `xray_tech` (the feared onboarding blocker is **absent**). Billing tab is honest ("managed by your agency"). `permissions.js` MATRIX correctly models all 5 roles with `xray_tech` narrowly scoped. Operator console + clinic credentials are correctly gated behind `isOperator`.

**Gaps:**
- **[A-1] SB-8 — Settings silent data loss** (whitelist). Same root cause silently drops the Organization tab's `industry` + brand `primary_color`, and AI settings + KB uploads (also partly localStorage).
- **[A-2] SB-9 / SB-10 / SB-11 — Auth/Join dead-ends** (forgot-password, password rule, re-clicked invite).
- **[A-3] HIGH / M — No page-level `can()` gate.** Role gating exists ONLY in the sidebar nav filter; the App.jsx render block renders on `page===X` alone (`App.jsx:795–803,1059–1084`). An `xray_tech`/`assistant` reaching `/reports`, `/finance`, `/automations` by URL or Cmd+K loads the full page — e.g. **xray_tech sees clinic-wide financials.** *(RLS still protects raw data, but the surface is exposed and several pages compute/aggregate client-side.)*
- **[A-4] HIGH / M — CommandPalette (Cmd+K) is unfiltered and points at dead routes.** Lists `pipeline`/`tickets` nav targets and `create-deal`/`new-ticket` actions that **don't exist** → blank screen (`CommandPalette.jsx:41–56`); ignores role permissions entirely (xray_tech → Finance/Settings/Reports). A power-user owner will try Cmd+K first.
- **[A-5] HIGH / M — Owner cannot change a member's role or deactivate them after they accept.** Team list shows name + role badge only — no email, no role change, no remove; role is "operator-managed" (`SettingsPage.jsx:656–675`). A mis-assigned role needs operator intervention.
- **[A-6] HIGH / S — AI floating button is shown to every role** (`App.jsx:1201`) with no gate. *Mitigation found:* the backend `api/ai/chat.js` **is** now role-gated to owner/doctor/receptionist (CLAUDE.md "no role gate" note is stale), so xray_tech/assistant get a 403 — but the button still appears and lets owner/doctor/reception spend uncontrolled AI budget with no per-user cap.
- **[A-7] HIGH / S — Auth screen leaks raw Supabase errors, is English-only in Arabic mode, and shows a "Create test account" button on the production login** — unprofessional for a paying clinic. Verify `VITE_OPERATOR_CONTACT` is set or the "contact the operator" fallback is a dead message.
- **[A-8] MEDIUM/HIGH / — DocsPage is localStorage-only** (separate from the real `documents` table), per-device/unshared, plus `contentEditable` + `dangerouslySetInnerHTML` with no sanitization (`javascript:`-insertable links) — self-XSS now, stored-XSS if ever shared.
- **[A-9] LOW — `permissions.js` header comment says "four canonical roles"** but ships five (omits `xray_tech`) — misleads the next dev syncing RLS.

### 3.5 Mobile

Saif expects **phone + tablet** (per V1.5 scope). The recent responsive sweep (PR #53) fixed touch targets, modals, dental restack, and Settings — but whole surfaces remain desktop-only.

- **[M-1] HIGH / L — AppointmentsPage is desktop-only** (fixed 240px + 340px panels) — the calendar is the front desk's primary screen.
- **[M-2] HIGH / L — TasksPage is desktop-only** (`repeat(4,1fr)`, drag-drop dead on touch).
- **[M-3] HIGH / L — FormsPage builder is desktop-only.**
- **[M-4] MEDIUM — "Today's Schedule" on the dashboard and various lists are unbounded** — 50+ appts = a giant scroll on a phone.

### 3.6 Integrations

**What works well (backend):** Outbound WhatsApp via Meta Cloud API is production-grade — role-gated, rate-limited (1000/day), per-org creds, persists message + conversation (`api/whatsapp/send.js`). Inbound webhook does per-org HMAC verify, matches patient by phone, persists inbound (`api/webhooks/whatsapp.js`). `api/social-fetch.js` is now owner-gated.

**Gaps:**
- **[I-1] SB-3 / SB-4 — Dead inbox + no automation engine** (the two biggest GHL-parity gaps; see blockers).
- **[I-2] SB-12 — IntegrationsPage is 100% fake** — shows WhatsApp + Gmail "Connected" out of the box; Connect/Disconnect flips in-memory state only. It **lies about connection status** (`IntegrationsPage.jsx:5–33`). Three different Settings tabs tell three contradictory stories about where integrations are managed.
- **[I-3] SB-1 — GHL import broken** against the live schema.
- **[I-4] HIGH / L — Forms persistence + public route missing** — real `forms`/`form_submissions` tables exist but are never used; FormsPage is localStorage-only; `/form/:id` 404s; "Submit" posts nowhere. Digital intake/consent (a classic dental front-desk need) doesn't work.
- **[I-5] HIGH — No SMS / no Twilio anywhere; no message templates, broadcast, or segmentation.** WhatsApp-only. Owner can't self-configure WhatsApp (operator-only `org_secrets`).
- **[I-6] MEDIUM / S — `api/admin/payments.js` is stale/broken** — queries `organizations`/`contacts`/`payments.user_id`, none of which exist in the current schema; gates on a `profiles.is_super_admin` column not in `schema.sql`. Operator-only, not clinic-facing. **Deleting it frees a Vercel function slot** (relevant to the 12/12 ceiling).
- **[I-7] LOW — `api/social-fetch.js` hardcodes `Access-Control-Allow-Origin: https://velo-crm-coral.vercel.app`** — breaks under any other deployment domain; depends on fragile third-party scrape APIs.

### 3.7 Data-integrity items to resolve **before** the GHL import

- **[D-1] MEDIUM / XS but important — IQD unit conflict.** CLAUDE.md + schema comment say IQD is stored in **fils**, but `money.js:17–24` stores **whole dinars**. Self-consistent today, but if the import assumes fils, historical IQD lands 1000× off. **Decide and align doc+code before importing Saif's payments.**
- **[D-2] Verify Inventory visibility.** One stream reports `inventory` is **not a key in the permissions MATRIX**, so `can(role,'inventory','r')` is false and the sidebar link is **hidden for everyone** — a complete, working feature shipped invisible (fix = one MATRIX entry, XS). Another stream saw it working with a sidebar banner. **Verify in prod**; if hidden, this is a free Saif win (the X-ray dept wants supply tracking).

## 4. Competitor comparison

What the market leaders do that Velo doesn't — and where Velo already wins. (Per the guardrail: gaps that *every* competitor also lacks in the Iraq cash-clinic context are noted but not over-prioritized.)

### vs **GHL (GoHighLevel)** — Saif's *current* system; the bar to beat
GHL is a generic marketing CRM, not dental. Saif uses it for: **automated appointment reminders, 2-way WhatsApp/SMS, follow-up sequences, contact tagging per doctor, forms, and a unified conversations inbox.**

| Capability Saif uses in GHL today | Velo status |
|---|---|
| Automated appointment reminders | ❌ SB-4/SB-5 (manual `wa.me` only) |
| 2-way messaging inbox | ❌ SB-3 (inbox dead in prod) |
| Follow-up / nurture sequences | ❌ SB-4 (Automations is mock) |
| Contact tagging per doctor | ⚠️ partial — `primary_doctor_id` filter exists; import (SB-1) must map GHL tags → it |
| Forms with real submissions | ❌ I-4 |
| Pipelines / lead stages | ❌ none (patients have no stage concept) |

**→ On Saif's daily GHL workflows, Velo currently loses.** These are the parity gaps that decide the July experience. **Where Velo crushes GHL:** real dental charting, treatment plans, X-ray imaging, prescriptions, per-tooth clinical history — GHL has none of it.

### vs **Open Dental** (open-source, common in many markets)
Has, that Velo lacks: **recall/continuing-care automation, built-in lab-case manager** (track crowns/dentures sent/received/due), **operatory/chair scheduling**, ledger + A/R + statements, insurance, **eClipboard digital intake forms**, automated eReminders/eConfirmations, perio charting. Velo matches: charting, tx plans, imaging, documents. Velo's edge: modern bilingual UI, WhatsApp-native, cloud multi-tenant.

### vs **Dentrix** (US market leader)
Has, that Velo lacks: **production-by-provider reporting** (SB-7), full ledger/A/R + statements (SB-6), insurance claims/eligibility, **continuing-care/recall**, operatory scheduling, e-prescribing, patient portal, eServices reminders/online booking. Dentrix's strengths are insurance + A/R + recall — two of which (A/R, recall) Velo must address; insurance is lower priority for an Iraq cash clinic.

### vs **Curve Hero** (modern cloud-based — closest philosophical peer)
Has, that Velo lacks: integrated patient comms with **automated reminders + 2-way text**, **online forms** that persist, billing/A/R, insurance, reporting dashboards, operatory scheduling, patient portal. Curve shows the target shape for a modern cloud dental PMS: Velo's clinical depth is comparable, but Curve's **comms + scheduling + financial-ledger** layers are where Velo is thin.

### Cross-competitor gaps Velo shares (note, don't over-prioritize for July)
- **Insurance claim automation** — weak/absent across the board for the Iraq market; Saif is likely cash-only. **Confirm, then deprioritize.**
- Patient portal / online self-booking — none of these are table-stakes for Saif's July launch.

**Synthesis:** Velo is **ahead of GHL on clinical** and **behind Open Dental/Dentrix/Curve on recall, A/R, scheduling, lab, and comms-automation.** The fastest path to "better than GHL for Saif" is to close the **comms + import + per-doctor-reporting + A/R** gaps on top of the strong clinical spine — not to chase insurance or a patient portal.
